Attire

Dress code

To help us create a beautiful, cohesive atmosphere against the scenic backdrop of Tagaytay, we kindly ask our loved ones to follow our wedding dress code guidelines below.

The Entourage

Principal Sponsors Dignified formal attire: elegant gowns for the ladies, and classic suits for the gentlemen, following our neutral tones.

Bridesmaids Beautiful, elegant floor-length beige dresses.

Groomsmen Sharp, formal black tuxedos paired with a white long-sleeved button-up shirt, a classic black suit pants, and a refined beige tie.

Our Guests

We kindly invite our guests to dress in formal or semi-formal attire within our soft, romantic color palette ranging strictly from ivory white to beige.

Ladies Ivory white, cream, or light beige formal dresses, maxi gowns, or elegant pantsuits.

Gentlemen Light beige or cream long-sleeved button-up shirts, pairing beautifully with beige, tan, or neutral trousers.
